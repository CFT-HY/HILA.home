Hila (lattice in Finnish) is a C++ programming framework and suite of Lattice Field Theory aimed at HPC simulations.

Purpose of HILA: to make writing applications straightforward and intuitive, while producing optimized executables for different (super)computing platforms (parallelization with MPI, GP-GPU computing with Cuda or ROCm/HIP, AVX vectorization, etc.). Write once -- run anywhere.
