HILA (lattice in Finnish) is a C++ programming framework and suite of Lattice Field Theory targeted on HPC simulations.

Purpose of HILA: to make writing CFT applications straightforward and intuitive, while producing optimized executables for different (super) computing platforms.

The supported accelerations are parallelization with MPI, GP-GPU computing with Cuda or ROCm/HIP and AVX vectorization etc.

Write Once -- Run Anywhere
