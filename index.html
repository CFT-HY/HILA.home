--- layout: home background: '/img/AxionStringNetwork.jpg' --- Hila (lattice in Finnish) is a C++ programming framework and suite of [Lattice Field Theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lattice_field_theory) aimed at HPC simulations. Purpose: To make writing applications straightforward and intuitive, while producing optimized executables for different (super)computing platforms (parallelization with MPI, GPU computing with Cuda or HIP, AVX vectorization, etc.). **Write once -- run anywhere**.
