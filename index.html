Hila (lattice in Finnish) is a C++ programming framework and suite of Lattice Field Theory aimed at HPC simulations.

Purpose of HILA: to make writing applications straightforward and intuitive, while producing optimized executables for different (super)computing platforms (parallelization with MPI, GPU computing with Cuda or HIP, AVX vectorization, etc.).

Write once -- run anywhere
